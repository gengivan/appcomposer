Publishing

How to publish in Graasp

Translated Apps can be published in the Graasp platform easily. This way, students will be able to access your app from within a Graasp space.

To publish it, go to your Graasp space. Then, simply create a new application and input the provided Graasp publish link as an URL. The link is the following:

{{ link }}

How to publish in other container

You can publish the App in any place that supports standard OpenSocial applications.

Because Google's OpenSocial internationalization scheme doesn't support translations unique for specific groups of people (such as specific ages), a unique app is generated for each language and group combination.

The specific XML specification of the App for the language and group you chose is here:

{{ link }}

The XML file in the link will be updated automatically each time your translation is updated. Copy the link wherever appropriate to refer to your App.
